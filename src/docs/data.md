# Data

Your entries belong to you. They live on your device first, and if you turn on sync, the only person who can read them is you — signed in with your vault password.

### Sync across devices

Sign in and create a **vault** with a password. After that, every entry is encrypted before it leaves your device, and only unlocked again when another signed-in device opens the vault with the same password.

The password is yours alone — it never reaches the server, so we can't see it, and we can't recover it for you.

> If you lose your vault password, the entries on the server can't be brought back, but the copies on your device are still safe.

Sync just pauses until you set up a new vault.

### Export

From **Settings → Data**, download a JSON file containing every note, task, mood, and intention. Save it wherever you keep important things — a backup drive, a password manager attachment, your home folder. That's your portable copy of the journal, sitting safely outside the app.

### Coming later

Importing from a previous export isn't supported yet. More backup and restore options will land over time.
