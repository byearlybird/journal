# Entries

Everything you write is an entry. There are three kinds, each suited to a different shape of thought:

- ○ [**Notes**](/docs/notes) — open reflections, ideas, half-thoughts.
- □ [**Tasks**](/docs/tasks) — small actions for today.
- ☆ [**Intentions**](/docs/intentions) — a broader theme for the month.

### Where you'll find them

**Today** is the default view — a blank page for the day, plus anything you've written so far.

**All entries** is everything you've ever written, grouped by month, for when you want to look back.

**Pinned notes**, **open tasks**, and your **current intention** all live in the sidebar.

Filter any of these views by [**label**](/docs/labels) if you want to narrow to one part of life at a time.

### Updating an entry

Two ways, depending on how much you want to do:

- **From the list** — tap the icon next to an entry to flip a task between states or pin a note. Quick changes, no extra screens.
- **From the detail view** — tap the entry itself to open it. The detail view shows the full content with options to edit, delete, defer, and anything else the type supports.

### Creating one

The **+** button in the top right opens a blank page. Toggle between Note and Task at any time — Intentions work a little differently and live in the sidebar prompt at the start of each month.
