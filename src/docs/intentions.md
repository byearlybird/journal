# Intentions

A broad direction for the month ahead. What matters right now? Give a name to a theme, priority, or focus — and let it guide how you show up each day.

> ☆ _Be more present with the people I care about_

### Why set an Intention?

Without a clear focus, days blur together. Setting an intention gives your time a direction — not a rigid plan, but a compass for what actually matters this month.

### Try this

- Set one broad theme or focus for the month
- Keep it broad enough to apply to all kinds of days
- Revisit it when you feel scattered or off track

### Avoid this

- Setting more than one — pick the one that matters most
- Making it too specific or measurable. Intentions are themes, not targets
- Overthinking it. A word or a sentence is enough
