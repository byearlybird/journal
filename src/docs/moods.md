# Moods

A quick read on how you're feeling right now. One slider, from unpleasant to pleasant.

> ◇ _Somewhat pleasant_

### Why log Moods?

Noticing how you feel is its own kind of reflection. A mood is a low-friction way to check in with yourself.

Over time, the pattern matters more than any single entry. Looking back at a stretch of moods — alongside your notes and tasks — can surface rhythms you wouldn't have noticed otherwise.

### Logging one

Open the create dialog, switch to the Mood toggle (◇), and drag the slider until it matches how you're feeling. Add a label if you want to tie it to a part of your life — work, home, health — and save.

### Try this

- Check in more than once if your day shifts — moods are snapshots, not summaries
- Pair a mood with a label when you want to see how a particular part of life is trending

### Avoid this

- Treating it like a score to optimize. There's no good or bad number
- Overthinking the slider position. Roughly right is right enough
